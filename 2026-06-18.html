⚡ CLAUDE CODE TIP OF THE DAY

Use parallel subagents for research in half the time.

When you're researching a big decision in Claude Code, spawn 3 subagents at once instead of asking sequentially. Try: "Spawn 3 subagents in parallel: one researches Klaviyo pricing, one researches Omnisend, one researches Drip. Return a comparison table." Three searches, same wall time. Works for supplier vetting, tool comparisons, content research — anything you'd normally ask in three separate rounds.
