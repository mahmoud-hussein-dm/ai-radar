CLAUDE CODE TIP OF THE DAY — Verifiable Done-Criteria

Stop giving Claude vague end-states. Instead, specify a machine-checkable spec:

BAD: "Write 5 product descriptions for RABBIO."

GOOD: "Write 5 product descriptions for RABBIO. Each must be under 120 words, include [brand_name], at least one sensory detail, and end with a CTA. Return a JSON array with keys: title, description, word_count. Self-check word_count before returning."

Claude will actually count and retry failing entries rather than guessing. Use this pattern for any batch output: ad copy, email sequences, meta descriptions. Your review time drops from 20 mins to 2 mins.
