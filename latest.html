💡 CLAUDE CODE TIP OF THE DAY

USE VERIFIABLE DONE-CRITERIA IN EVERY PROMPT

Instead of: "Write product descriptions for these 10 items"

Write: "Write product descriptions for these 10 items. You are done when: (1) all 10 have a title, 3-5 bullet features, and a CTA under 50 words, (2) you've done a word-count check on each, (3) you've flagged any that sound generic or off-brand."

Verifiable done-criteria stop Claude from declaring success too early. They also give you a natural checkpoint to review output before Claude moves on to the next task — essential for batch ecom work.
