🧠 CLAUDE CODE TIP OF THE DAY

Verifiable Done-Criteria
════════════════════════════════

When you hand Claude a task, end your prompt with:
"Before you start: what will I be able to observe that confirms this is working correctly?"

Claude will write the verification steps into its plan. That turns vague output into auditable output — you know exactly what to check, and Claude knows what it's being held to. Works equally well in Claude.ai, Claude Code, and any agent loop.
