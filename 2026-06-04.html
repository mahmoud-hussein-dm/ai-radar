CLAUDE CODE TIP OF THE DAY — Parallel Subagents & waitingFor

When you spawn parallel subagents for a batch task (scraping 10 supplier sites, generating 50 product descriptions, auditing a full email flow), use:

claude agents --json | jq '.[].waitingFor'

The new waitingFor field (Claude Code 2.1.x) shows exactly which agent is blocked and why — no more waiting out a timeout to find the stalled subagent in a long-running multi-agent pipeline.
